Our Story

Fire, patience
& provenance

Java Lava is born from the volcanic soils that grow the world's most prestigious coffee. We take ethically sourced Arabica, cold-brew it slowly, and marry it with the warmth of Caribbean rum and the earthy sweetness of blue agave.

The result is a liqueur of rare depth — bold enough to anchor a cocktail, refined enough to sip neat beside the fire.
