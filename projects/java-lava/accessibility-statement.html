Accessibility Statement

Last updated on 11/25

General Commitment

Johnny Rocks Spirit Co. (doing business as "Java Lava") is dedicated to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to ensure our website is inclusive and usable for all customers, regardless of ability.

2. Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA.

The Java Lava website is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. "Partially conformant" means that some parts of the content do not yet fully conform to the accessibility standard, though we are actively working to close these gaps.

3. Measures to Support Accessibility

Johnny Rocks Spirit Co. takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of the Java Lava website:

  • Strategic Integration: Including accessibility as a key requirement in our internal mission statement.
  • Vendor Procurement: Integrating accessibility requirements into our procurement practices when selecting third-party tools.
  • Continuous Education: Providing accessibility training for our staff and content creators.
  • Quality Assurance: Regularly reviewing our website to identify and fix usability issues.

4. Technical Specifications

Accessibility of the Java Lava website relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.

5. Third-Party Disclaimer (AccelPay & Integration)

Please be aware that our website utilizes third-party tools and integrations to provide e-commerce functionality, specifically the AccelPay network for age verification and payment processing.

While we encourage our vendors to comply with accessibility standards, Johnny Rocks Spirit Co. does not control the coding or accessibility features of these third-party tools. If you encounter barriers while using the checkout or age-verification portals provided by AccelPay, please let us know so we can forward this feedback to the vendor, but please understand these elements are outside our direct control.

6. Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of the Java Lava website, there may be some limitations. Please contact us if you observe an issue not listed below:

  • User-Generated Content: Images or reviews uploaded by users may not always have text alternatives (Alt Text).
  • Archived Documents: Older PDF documents (if any) may not work with current screen reader software.

Alternative Service: If you are unable to navigate our website or complete an order due to a disability, we are happy to assist you via email or other non-digital means to ensure you can still access our products.

7. Feedback and Contact Information

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the Java Lava website. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers:

  • E-mail: info@javalava.rocks
  • Postal Address: Johnny Rocks Spirit Co., 10123 SE Brittany Ct, Clackamas, OR 97015

We try to respond to feedback within 3-5 business days.
