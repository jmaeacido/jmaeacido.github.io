# Java Lava — Drink Art Prompt Pack

Interim images on the site are realistic, license-free stock (Pexels, free for commercial
use) used as **placeholders**. To produce the final, on-brand "highly realistic" renders,
run the prompts below in an image generator (Midjourney v6 / Flux / DALL·E / Firefly).

**How to swap in finals:** save each as the matching filename in `site/assets/cocktails/`
(e.g. `lava-martini.webp`) and the page picks it up automatically — no code change needed.

## Shared brand style (append to every prompt)
> dark moody editorial cocktail photography, premium spirits advertising, deep volcanic
> black background, molten-gold and ember-orange accent light, subtle rising embers and
> warm bokeh, condensation and craft ice, shallow depth of field, 85mm, soft rim light,
> ultra-realistic, high detail, no text, no watermark --ar 4:5

Optional: include the bottle — *"a dark bottle with a gold volcano label labelled 'Java
Lava' softly out of focus behind the glass."*

---

### 1. Lava Martini  → `lava-martini.webp`
Espresso martini in a chilled coupe, glossy crema top, three coffee beans centered, a thin
thread of caramel; warm gold backlight. + shared style.

### 2. The Volcanic  → `the-volcanic.webp`
A dark coffee liqueur served neat over one large clear rock in a heavy rocks glass, orange
twist, amber glow, wisp of smoke; rustic dark stone surface. + shared style.

### 3. Ember & Cream  → `ember-cream.webp`
Tall glass of dark cold-brew coffee liqueur with a marbled float of vanilla cream swirling
into the dark liquid, layered, condensation on glass. + shared style.

### 4. Lava & Tonic  → `lava-tonic.webp`
Long highball of dark coffee liqueur lengthened with tonic, lots of clear ice, a fat orange
wheel, fine bubbles, backlit ember glow. + shared style.

### 5. Volcano Flat White  → `flat-white.webp`
A boozy flat white in a stoneware cup, latte-art rosetta dusted with cocoa, steam rising,
warm low light, cozy dark café. + shared style.

### 6. Affogato Eruption  → `affogato.webp`
A scoop of vanilla gelato in a glass coupe with espresso and dark coffee liqueur poured over,
melting, glistening, single spoon, dramatic side light. + shared style.

### 7. Featured / hero moodshot  → `featured-moody.webp`
Hero composition: espresso martini in the foreground with the Java Lava bottle and a few
coffee cherries and tropical leaves behind, faint volcano silhouette and ember haze, cinematic.
+ shared style --ar 3:4
